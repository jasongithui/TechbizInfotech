Discover the power of Dynamics 365 Business Central for Manufacturing

Successful manufacturers need to have agile shop floors, be able to trade efficiently via multiple channels, have up-to-the-minute inventory & warehouse movement control whilst all the time delivering on time and in full (OTIF) backed up by faultless customer service.

Relying on multiple, disjointed departmental systems, error-prone spreadsheets or paper-based processes just wont cut it anymore!

Make the move to an integrated solution for order management, finance, production, distribution and service with Dynamics 365 Business Central.

Seamlessly integrate Dynamics 365 Sales, Marketing & Customer Service (aka Dynamics CRM) plus proven, sector specific apps for EDI, eCommerce, your shop floor, warehouse and shipment management for a single, cohesive solution for growing manufacturers.

And all supported by the manufacturing and distribution industry expertise of #TeamTecman!
